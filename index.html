Tremor Monitoring System

This ASP.NET MVC 4 project creates a long term monitoring system that continuously monitors the frequency, pattern and severity of a patient’s tremors in the legs. This project would allow doctors to monitor the tremors of a patient by measuring their leg movements, such as acceleration, speed and orientation. The doctor can use the measured data to provide better diagnostics and treatments for Parkinson's disease patients.
